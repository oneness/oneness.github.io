# Repository

Personal blog at birkey.co. Built with org-static-blog. Posts are Org
files in =posts/=, drafts in =drafts/=, static assets in =static/=.

# Blog writing

The blog writing profile lives at =docs/blog-writing-profile.org=. It
captures voice, tone, structure, formatting conventions, recurring
themes, and an idea tracker for birkey.co blog posts.

When drafting or brainstorming a new blog post:

1. Read the writing profile first to align with the author's voice and
   style.
2. Propose the idea in the "Ideas and Drafts" section of the profile.
   Include status, summary, key points, and links to related posts.
3. Draft the post as an Org file in =drafts/= matching the naming and
   formatting conventions described in the profile.
4. After a post is published, move it to =posts/= and update the
   status in the profile. Add any new themes or patterns that emerged.

# Principles

Make every change easy to verify. Read existing posts before writing
new ones to stay consistent with voice and tone.

Do not perform destructive operations by default. Prefer append-only
edits. Do not overwrite or delete existing posts without explicit
instruction.
